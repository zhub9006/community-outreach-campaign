## 🎯 Campaign Strategy Notes

### Outreach Format Recommendations by Weather

- **Hot & Sunny (Mon–Wed):** Focus on **indoor venues** (libraries, community centers) with AC. If outdoor, schedule early morning (8–11am) or evening (5–8pm). Provide water stations, shade canopies, and cooling areas.
- **Comfortable & Dry (Thu):** **Ideal for outdoor events.** Pioneer Courthouse Square and Hawthorne street-side outreach are prime options.
- **Rainy (Fri–Sat):** **Indoor only.** Libraries, community centers, and Revolution Hall are the go-to venues.
- **Mild & Low Rain (Sun):** **Good for mixed indoor/outdoor.** Armory in Pearl District offers both. Belmont Library for SE.

### Venue Booking Priority

1. **Pioneer Courthouse Square** — Requires event permit from Portland Parks & Recreation. Book early — it's a popular venue.
2. **Revolution Hall** — Contact for availability; large capacity venue.
3. **Portland Center Stage at The Armory** — Check with PCS for community event availability.
4. **Central Library & Belmont Library** — Multnomah County library meeting rooms can be reserved online through the library system.
5. **Portland City Hall** — Contact City of Portland for public meeting room reservations.
